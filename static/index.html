The 15-Criterion Predictive Framework

Based on Perplexity Research analysis of 15 stocks that delivered 1,000%+ returns in 10 years or less. Scores update daily (quantitative via FMP) and weekly (qualitative via Gemma AI). Not investment advice.

📊 Quantitative (Daily, FMP API)

  1. Revenue Growth >25% YoY
  2. Gross Margin >50%
  3. FCF Positive
  4. Gross Margin Expanding
  5. Low Valuation vs Growth (P/S ÷ growth %)
  6. Strong Balance Sheet (cash > debt)
  7. Insider Ownership >10%
  8. FCF Per Share Growing

🤖 Qualitative (Weekly, Gemma AI)

  1. TAM > $50B
  2. Recurring Revenue Model
  3. Network Effects / Moat
  4. Founder-Led
  5. NRR >120%
  6. Category Creator
  7. No Single Customer >15%

📚 Research Basis

  1. 15 companies with 1,000%+ returns (1986–2025)
  2. Study of 464 multibaggers over 15 years
  3. Low FCF yield + low valuation predicts returns better than growth rate
  4. Best entry near 52-week lows, not highs
  5. Insider buying during downgrades = strongest contrarian signal
  6. Share count growth >10%/yr destroys per-share value
  7. NRR >120% = #1 software quality metric
